Heart Failure
- DESCRIPTION

  1. Decreased cardiac output
  2. Impaired respiratory function
    1. ineffective breathing pattern
    2. ineffective airway clearance
    3. impaired gas exchange
  3. Altered fluid and electrolyte balance
    1. fluid volume excess
    2. third-spacing of fluid
    3. hyponatremia
  4. Altered nutrition: less than body requirements
  5. Risk for impaired tissue integrity
  6. Activity intolerance
  7. Sleep pattern disturbance
  8. Risk for trauma: falls
  9. Potential complications
    1. renal insufficiency
    2. cardiac dysrhythmias
    3. acute pulmonary edema
    4. thromboembolism
    5. cardiogenic shock
  10. Fear/Anxiety
  11. Knowledge deficit, Ineffective management of therapeutic regimen, or Altered health maintenance
